About EPIC Rentals

We're five friends from Chennai who couldn't justify owning a PS5 at full price — so we built the next best thing. EPIC Rentals lets you experience the full PS5 gaming thrill at home, for a fraction of the cost, with zero commitment.

Started small with 2 consoles and a mission: make premium gaming accessible to everyone in Chennai. We deliver, you play, we pick it back up. Simple as that.

Big plans ahead — more consoles, more cities, more games. Stay tuned.
